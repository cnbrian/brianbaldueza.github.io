Sphere for Business
MICROSITE DESIGN
01 —
background

Sphere uses a microsite for business to outline the different structures and solutions that it can offer to existing and potential clients.

Tools: Figma, Webflow

02 —
Problem

The main website including the Business microsite has been in need of a design refresh to match current design trends, to have a modernized look, and to have concise information that can be easily understood by all.

02 —
COLOUR AND FONTS

The old microsite only used one font and weight along with two colours, white and blue. To add variety, I added an orange and a green to the branding guidelines to improve contrast and saturation throughout the microsite.
The new and modern sans-serif fonts were also added to introduce hierarchy to the website text.

03 —
DESIGN METHOD

After multiple iterations based on feedback, the main design decision I took was to maintain the core ethos of the old design, and give it a modernized redesign in line with current design trends and standards.

REFLECTIONS

This microsite re-design was my first professional project within a user experience capacity. Although it was a solo endeavour, the feedback I received from my peers, seniors, and management were critical to the design decisions I made.

Despite the challenges of designing and building an entire microsite alone, it was a tremendous learning experience about how to re-imagine and improve design systems for a specific purpose.
